## Introduction to Application

Everyday small choices can take up more mental energy than people expect. Oracle Map is a Flutter mobile app designed to help with this problem. It focuses on small daily decisions such as what to eat, whether to go out, whether to rest, or how to spend time after work. Instead of acting like an AI assistant that gives a direct answer, Oracle Map works as a quiet reflection tool. It helps users pause, interact, and notice their own reaction to a result.

The app includes four decision tools: a coin flip, a Tarot draw, a dice roll, and fortune sticks. These tools are subjective by design. They are not meant to replace judgement. They are meant to help users explore what they may already want. The app also includes optional saving to a personal Decision Map. When a record is saved, the user can add a question, a mood, and a final decision. The app can also attach weather, location, and an optional photo. Firebase stores these records with anonymous authentication, and OpenWeatherMap provides environmental context. In this way, Oracle Map links a small personal decision to a real moment in the connected environment.
