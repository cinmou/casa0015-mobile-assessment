## Introduction to Application

Everyday small choices take up a lot of mental energy. Oracle Map is a Flutter app designed to solve this problem. Most apps use AI to give direct answers. Oracle Map works differently. It serves as a quiet reflection tool.
The app offers four simple options: a coin flip, a Tarot draw, a dice roll, and fortune sticks. Users physically shake their device to get a result. This movement makes the digital experience feel real.
After making a choice, users can save it to a personal 'Decision Map'. The app automatically records the current weather, the physical location, and an optional photo. This links the digital decision directly to the physical environment. Firebase stores all these records anonymously to protect user privacy.
Oracle Map never makes the final choice for the user. It simply gives them a moment to pause, think, and figure out what they really want.
