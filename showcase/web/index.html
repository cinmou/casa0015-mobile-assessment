Oracle Map

Oracle Map is a mobile app for small daily choices. It uses simple physical actions, optional records, weather, and location to help users reflect on the choices they make.

Screenshots Demo media

What it does

Users can flip a coin, draw tarot, roll dice, or draw a fortune stick. They can then save the moment to a personal Decision Map.

Why it exists

Small choices can still take energy. This app gives users a calm way to pause, make a choice, and look back at their own patterns later.
